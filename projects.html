LingCopilot: An LLM-Empowered Quantitative Trading System

Fintech

Our flagship innovation, Lingcopliot, leverages AI to transform natural language concepts into backtestable, executable strategies throughout the trading lifecycle. Diga: Graph Diffusion for Explainable Anti‑Money Laundering

Banking

The Diga project is a flagship initiative under the Joint Laboratory of HKUST and WeBank, aiming to achieve high-recall, interpretable anti-money laundering monitoring using Graph Neural Networks. Ourteam introduces a novel guided graph diffusion method based on subgraph reconstruction to identify suspicious accounts in transaction networks at a scale of 200 million accounts.

InLN: Incremental Leveling Network for Dynamic Ads

Advertising
Image / Video

A spatiotemporal graph neural network for real-time computational advertising. InLN incrementally updates ad representations as new user interaction data streams in, capturing evolving interests without costly full retraining. Knowledge-aware leveling integrates domain knowledge graphs for cold-start resilience. Published at KDD 2024.
